California Balcony Laws

SB-721 (Apartments)

  • Applies to buildings with 3+ rental units
  • Requires inspection every 6 years
  • Covers balconies, decks, walkways, and stairs
  • Must be completed by licensed inspector

SB-326 (Condos/HOAs)

  • Applies to common interest developments
  • Inspection required every 9 years
  • Covers elevated structures 6+ feet high
  • Non-compliance can result in fines
