SB-721

Apartments

SB-721 covers exterior elevated elements for multi-family buildings (3+ units). Inspections by licensed pros, repair timelines, and report retention are required.

  • Applies to buildings with 3+ units
  • Requires licensed inspector
  • Mandatory repair timelines
  • Report retention required
SB-326

Condos/HOAs

SB-326 focuses on condo/HOA EEEs; periodic inspections, reporting to the association, and repair follow-through are required.

  • Applies to condos and HOAs
  • Periodic inspection schedule
  • Association reporting required
  • Repair follow-through mandatory
